Key Concepts: Verifying Information

  • Always **check sources**. Trust official reports or well-known journalists. If Fahima hears something important (like a health center), she **verifies** by contacting the source. Trusted news keeps people safe.
  • Be careful with social media **rumors**. Not all posts are true. Fahima did the right thing by waiting to verify. You can also check official websites or ask community leaders.
  • **Empowerment through media:** By using media wisely, women can stay informed and help others. Being critical and asking questions helps uphold the mission of media programs that aim to connect women to education.
