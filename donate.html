Support AFGWomen

Your donation helps provide vital resources and education to Afghan women seeking a better future. As a non-profit run by volunteers, we rely on generous contributions to keep our courses accessible and our platform growing. With your support, we can cover technology costs, develop new courses, and reach women in even the most remote areas.

Donation Options

  • €25 – Provides course materials for one woman.
  • €50 – Sponsors a full online course for one student.
  • €100 – Expands our platform capabilities to reach more learners.
  • Any amount – Every contribution helps us move closer to our vision.
