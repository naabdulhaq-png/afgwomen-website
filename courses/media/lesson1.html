Key Concepts: Forms of Media

  • **Media** includes TV, radio, newspapers, and the internet. It delivers news and information to people. For example, Begum Radio and TV broadcast educational programs into Afghan homes.
  • **Radio** is especially accessible. UNESCO reports that Afghan media projects broadcast lessons on radio for women and girls. A good example is **Tabasum**, a weekly TV show from Begum Media that creates a â€œsafe space to learn and shareâ€ for Afghan women.
  • TV and radio **programs** often have a host or announcer who speaks.
