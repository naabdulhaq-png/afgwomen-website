Key Concepts: Hardware vs. Software

  • A **computer** is an electronic device that processes information. It comes in many forms (desktop, laptop, tablet). You can use a computer to write documents, send emails, or learn online.
  • **Hardware** refers to the parts you can touch (like the keyboard and screen). **Software** refers to programs that tell the hardware what to do.
  • To use a computer, you usually: turn it on with the power button, use the **mouse** to click icons, and use the **keyboard** to type. With time, you learn step by step.
