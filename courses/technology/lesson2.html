Key Concepts: Online Services and Opportunities

  • The **internet** on your phone opens many possibilities. You can send messages to relatives, read news, or take free classes. **Online learning** platforms (like Vision Online University) offer courses from home.
  • **Digital services:** The HesabPay example shows **digital banking** for aid. Other services exist: health apps may offer doctor advice, market prices, or language learning apps.
  • Always use **official websites**. For instance, UNICEF and WFP set up reliable apps. Avoid unknown links to stay safe.
