Our Partnerships

Collaborating with organizations around the world enables AFGWomen to extend our reach and resources. Our partners provide expertise, funding, and technology support. Some of our partners include:

  • Global Education Fund – supports online learning initiatives.
  • Tech4Women Alliance – provides technology and mentorship.
  • Local Community Centers – help identify and support students.

We are always open to new partnerships. Contact us if your organization would like to collaborate.
