Join Us as a Volunteer

Volunteers are the heart of AFGWomen. By donating your time and skills, you can contribute to a meaningful change. Whether you want to teach a course, mentor a student, or help with content creation, we welcome your support.

Volunteer Roles

  • Instructor: Teach language, technology, or business courses through our platform.
  • Mentor: Provide guidance and motivation to students on their learning journey.
  • Content Creator: Help develop course materials, videos, or learning activities.
  • Translator: Translate materials into Dari or Pashto to reach more learners.

Ready to get started? Contact Us to learn more about volunteer opportunities.
